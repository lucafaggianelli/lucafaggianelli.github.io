Let's offer intelligence to your dumb environment: Smart Environments with
Smart-M3 platform and wireless sensor networks.

I developed this project for the Wireless Sensor Networks course held at the
Electronic Engineering faculty of University of Bologna (Italy).

I created a *Smart House* deploying zigbee temperature sensors all around the
environments and building a software infrastructure based on
[Smart-M3](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Smart-M3), a Semantic Web
based platform for data sharing between hardware devices and software entities.
Data is stored as a OWL knowledge base.

I will add more details as soon as possible, in the meanwhile you can
[download Smart-M3](http://sourceforge.net/projects/smart-m3/).
The wireless sensor nodes are based on the Freescale MC1322x IC, I’m using the
official development kit.

Enjoy the presentation and don’t hesitate to contact me for further information
and most of all for your feedback!
